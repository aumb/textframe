Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 12, 2024

Introduction

Textframe ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension.

Information We Collect

Our extension collects and processes the following types of data:

  • Website Content: We process video content from YouTube pages you visit to enable our search functionality. This includes processing the content of videos to enable timestamp-based searching.
  • User Activity: We collect data about your interactions with our extension, including:
    • Search queries you enter into the extension
    • Interactions with video markers and the progress bar
    • Navigation through search results

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for:

  • Providing video content search functionality
  • Generating accurate timestamp markers
  • Improving the extension's performance and user experience
  • Technical troubleshooting

Data Storage and Security

We use Chrome's storage API to store minimal necessary data locally on your device. We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information and ensure the extension's functionality.

Third-Party Services

Our extension integrates with YouTube to provide its functionality. Please note that YouTube's own privacy policy applies to your use of their service.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Uninstall the extension at any time
  • Clear your local extension data through Chrome's settings
  • Request information about your data

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
